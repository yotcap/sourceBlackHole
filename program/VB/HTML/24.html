A Good Flashcard Game



          This is a very fun and adictive program. It can actually aide the user in developing their math skills. it's a sort of flashcard game. based on the chosen operation the computer will choose two random numbers and the user will have to answer the math problem within 5 secs., includes addition, subtracion, multiplication and division.
