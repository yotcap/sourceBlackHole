MemBrowser



          This program targets a specific application, and allows you to browse its memory. Writing is also possible. To target an application, place your mouse over its window and press Alt+T. To seek to a specific address, type the hex address in and press "Go." ^? and v? scan up or down, respectively, for values that are not zero. ^^ and vv scroll up and down a page. ^ and v scroll up and down a line. * refreshes the page.
