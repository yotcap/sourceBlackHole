3D Demonstration


          The purpose of the this application is to demonstrate the viewing in 3D world. It provides a complete graphical interface that let you easily interact with the application and helps you in drawing shapes. It let you see the every shape in three planes( XY, YZ, ZX) and also project the Points in Perspective projection plane. It also let you view the surafces that every shapes made in the three plane as well as in perspective projection plane. You can draw as many shapes as you like with a technique that i have demonstrated in the Help Menu.
