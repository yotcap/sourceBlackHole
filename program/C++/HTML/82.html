Journal



          This program is a personal journal program designed for a hostile environment. If you're like me, you cannot stand it when people look over your shoulder. This program automatically enables you to type out your own thoughts or memos and have them written to an organized filing system sorted by date. If you need to hide what you wrote and quick, just press tab. The change of focus to a small button on the lower left triggers a text flush to the file.
