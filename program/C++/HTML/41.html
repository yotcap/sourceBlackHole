Billing Software



          This code help user (specially the shop keeper) for creating the Bill. Which keep the record of its Product and Company Info. Detail of the Product : 1. How many product is there. 2. Rate of the Product. 3. Qty. Sold and left. It also provide further Function Like: 1. View Product in different Modes (One by one View and Tabular View (all product at once). 2. Search product by its ID and Name. 3. Inform you in which product there is no item. 4. Information of the Size use by the Company and the Product. 5. Updating of the Product. 6. Reset Product and Company Info.
